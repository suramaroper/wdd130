Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to be a one-stop place to provide information, resources, tools, and everything needed to all river rafting lovers.It is intent to drive more consumers for the products offered and build revenue to the White Waters store.

Audience

Our audience loves the outdoors, nature and adventure. Water is what they call home. They are people whose senses are sharp and they love connecting to nature. Also they have a great sense curiosity and love exploring new ways of living and learning about them.Earthly and nature colors attract them (blue, greens, browns)
